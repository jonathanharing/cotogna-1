A celebration
of rustic Italian cuisine

Cotogna offers a daily-changing menu of spit-roasted or grilled meats and fish, wood-oven pizzas and house-made pastas. Located in San Francisco's beautiful historic Jackson Square, Cotogna ('quince' in Italian) is a casual, lively venue and a complement to the adjoining Quince Restaurant, Chef Michael Tusk’s celebrated fine dining establishment.

Hours

Tuesday - Saturday
12:00 PM - 8:30 PM

All'aperto
(Outdoor dining)

Join us for outdoors for Cotogna Alla’perto (Italian for ‘open air’). San Francisco meets Tuscany with humble, rustic fare, spit-roasted or grilled in our hearth over a hardwood fire. Feast on a classic Bistecca alla Fiorentina or a ‘Griglia Mista’ with fresh Summer vegetables and salad bathed in Leccino olive oil, sea salt and Meyer lemons. Sop up the meat juices with some focaccia from our bakery and leave the plane tickets at home until better times.

Hours:
12:00 pm – 8:30 pm
Tuesday – Saturday
